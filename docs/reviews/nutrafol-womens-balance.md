# Nutrafol Women's Balance: A Six-Month Verdict

<p class="lead">
Nutrafol is one of the most recommended hair growth supplements among dermatologists. We put the Women's Balance formula — designed for women over 45 — through a comprehensive six-month evaluation.
</p>

## What Is Nutrafol Women's Balance?

Nutrafol Women's Balance is a daily nutraceutical specifically formulated for women experiencing hair thinning related to hormonal changes during perimenopause and menopause. Priced at roughly $88 per month, it sits firmly at the premium end of the supplement market.

The formula includes a proprietary blend of:

- **Synergen Complex** — saw palmetto, maca, astaxanthin
- **Marine collagen peptides**
- **Ashwagandha** (Sensoril extract)
- **Biotin** (3000mcg)
- **Tocotrienol complex** (vitamin E)
- **Curcumin** (Meriva formulation)

## The Clinical Evidence

Nutrafol has invested more in clinical research than most competitors. Two peer-reviewed studies (2018, 2020) demonstrated statistically significant improvements in hair growth, thickness, and coverage compared to placebo over six-month periods.

However, both studies were funded by Nutrafol, the sample sizes were modest (40-60 participants), and the primary measurement tool — phototrichogram analysis — has inherent variability.

> "Industry-funded research isn't automatically invalid, but it should be weighed alongside independent evidence, which in this case is limited." — HairVits Editorial Policy

## Our Experience

### Month 1-2: The Adjustment Period

No visible changes to hair quality. Some initial digestive adjustment in the first week, which resolved. The capsules are large (four per day), which may be a consideration for those who find pill-taking difficult.

### Month 3-4: Early Signs

Measurable reduction in hair shedding during wash days. Some new growth visible at the temples and parting line. Nails appeared stronger as a secondary benefit.

### Month 5-6: Meaningful Results

Noticeable improvement in overall hair density and texture. Hair felt thicker and more resilient. The changes were visible in photographs when compared to baseline images.

## Who Should Consider It?

Nutrafol Women's Balance is best suited for women over 45 experiencing diffuse thinning related to hormonal changes. If your hair loss is caused by nutritional deficiency, thyroid dysfunction, or other medical conditions, address those root causes first.
